Chayanon Lieng

Interior architect & Project manager

BArch Interior Architecture (Chulalongkorn University)
MA Public Archaeology (University College London)


I received my Bachelor of Architecture (5-year degree corresponding to Norway's Sivilarkitekt) from Chulalongkorn University.

In 2010, I received a full-scholarship from the Royal Thai Government to study museum exhibition development which resulted in a master degree in Public Archaeology from University College London. My MA dissertation on sensory design in museums and archaeological sites was accepted to be presented at the ICOM2013 conference. After graduation in 2011, I interned at Haley Sharpe Design, a renowned museum consultant firm in the UK. Then, in 2012, I started working at the National Science Museum of Thailand where I designed exhibitions and was in committees for new museum buildings across Thailand.

I also occasionally taught History of Asian Architecture and Introduction to Museum Design at university levels. Prior to starting Anomalist in 2014, I was an architectural consultant for UNESCO, designing workplace layouts and managing the relocation of UNESCO Asia-Pacific premises. more about me
